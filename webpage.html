Subsystems

Structure

Function: Houses and protects all internal electronic components during the high forces of launch and descent.

Power/Battery

Function: Provides continuous electrical energy to the flight computer, sensors, and radio throughout the mission.

Sensors (BMP/IMU)

Function: Collects vital atmospheric data (air pressure, temperature) and spatial orientation (acceleration, rotation).

Radio Transmitter

Function: Wirelessly beams live telemetry data and sensor readings back to the ground station in real-time.

Recovery System

Function: Dramatically slows the descent rate to ensure a safe landing and prevent damage to the payload upon impact.

Launch Vehicle (Rocket)

Function: The external shell and thruster that carries your CanSat payload into the sky!
